Frequently Asked Questions

Will I need to arrange transportation between my hotel, the cathedral, and the venue?

The church, the venue, and all of the hotels listed above are all walking distance from one another! Your total commute time between the three should be 5 - 10 minutes. You will not need to arrange for transportation of any kind.

Are children allowed?

Yes, but guests with children under the age of eight are strongly encouraged to take advantage of the nanny service provided by the venue. The cost is $34 USD for 5 hours and one nanny may be assigned up to 6 children.

Can I bring a guest?

Unfortunately, we can only admit those listed on your invitation. There is a strict limit on seating and all of the meals are accounted for according to the number of guests that RSVP'd. Thank you for your understanding.

Are you registered anywhere?

We ask only for your love and presence on our special day but if you were thinking of giving a gift, we would appreciate a cash gift to help us save for our new home. Thank you!

What will the weather be like in Morelia during this time of year?

In the summer months, Morelia is usually a perfect 70 - 80°F with clear skies and sunshine. However, it does occasionally rain so at least one rain jacket or coat is recommended.
