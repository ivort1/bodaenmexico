Travel Information

General Francisco Mujica International Airport
In order to travel to Mexico, you will need a valid U.S. passport. If you do not have one or if it has expired, please take the necessary steps to obtain or renew your passport. More information can be found on the U.S. Department of State website.
The currency in Mexico is the peso. As of June 2021, $1 Mexican peso is equivalent to around $19 US Dollars.
